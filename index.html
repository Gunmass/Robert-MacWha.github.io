OUR SOLUTION

Creating a concrete mixture that can passively absorb CO2 from our atmosphere

We are using modified cyanobacteria that rely on only carbon dioxide and hydrogen gas to survive and can survive temperatures in the range from 130 to 3 degrees Celsius. This is all possible through the incorporation of genes from certain bacteria that allow our bacteria to develop certain traits through gene editing and directed evolution.
