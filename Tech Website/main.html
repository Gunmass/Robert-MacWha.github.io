Main Attraction

Info on the ride [Materials]

This monument was built with the use of hundreds of popsicle sticks [4$], Grease[2], Motors[1], Wheels[4] and many Rubber Bands[~20]. The contraption is built to run on solar power due both the quantity of solar energy around us and because it produces the power in a much simpler manner. The solar panel (Located outside the ride) supplies power to our motor that turns a lift to bring the cars to the top of the Coaster.

Construction

During the process of construction, many problems were encountered. When building the initial incline we faced issues in the sense of the height of the base. We were having trouble figuring out how high it needed to be in order for it to run smoothly during the turn which follows. We eventually reached the conclusion of making the base 24.5 inches tall. When thinking about the turn we had trouble figuring out if the power from the incline would actually be enough for the car to successfully go around the turn. We eventually came to the (Incorrect) decision that with the height of the roller coaster it shouldn’t be too much of a problem. We spent countless hours in attempt to perfect the Roller Coaster but sadly it is non-functional. We think that the overall idea and creation was very well thought out but just lacked some key elements in order for it to work. Unfortunately, the ride is not successful even though Robert, Swarnava, Daisy and I put in large amounts of work. I think that this result occurred because we didn’t ever stop to think and ask ourselves the question will this actually work? I think that to improve we could have had better thought out our plans instead of just getting straight down to business and hoping for the best. We could have decided on a less complex amusement park ride such as a spinning ride because it requires much less precise detail and it seems much simpler. Building an entire Roller Coaster that works with solar or wind power is a difficult thing to do in the span of one month. Despite this challenge I think that my team and I did an incredible job at persevering and making it to the finished product.

Locations

This ride is located in the great city of Toronto, to the south of the main downtown area, in a well known park called Canada’s Wonder World. This is currently our newest attraction and we hope that even though the general public seems to have grown tired of waiting for its completion they will accept it in the end and realize how incredible it is to ride.
