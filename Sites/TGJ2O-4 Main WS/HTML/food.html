English Scones

English scones and american scones are two different foods entirely. In fact, looking at the two, the different ingredients and uses of them, it is safe to say that they should not share the name scone. They are simply miles apart. Personally, I love these english blueberry scones because of their light, fluffy texture and their sweet, almost tart taste. These scones are no exception. I often try to prepare them before bed during the summer, when I have time for that sort of thing, so I can pop them into the oven before breakfast.
