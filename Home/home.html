Timelapse of My Route

The route starts on Innes Rd. and travels west up Bearbrook Rd. From there it goes north past the Hornet's Nest and the Lafarge quarry. Then it turns towards Green's Creek and goes along the trail at the top of the hill all the way to the old raildoad bridge (not shown in the video).
